TIMELINE OF PHILOSOPHY

Ancient Foundation

Socrates - Known for questioning everything. He believed wisdom begins with admitting ignorance.

Plato - Student of Socrates. Proposed that reality exists beyond what we see.

Aristotle - Focused on logic, ethics, science, and observation. Influenced Western thought for centuries.

The Enlightment

René Descartes - Declared, “I think, therefore I am.” Sought certainty through reason.

John Locke - Argued that knowledge comes from experience and defended natural rights.

Immanuel Kant - Tried to unite reason and experience, redefining ethics and knowledge.
